ABOUT NORTHWEST SHUTTLEHUB

Your Premier Badminton Community Center

WELCOME TO NORTHWEST SHUTTLEHUB

Northwest ShuttleHub is your premier destination for badminton enthusiasts in the Seattle area. We provide a modern, state-of-the-art facility where players of all skill levels can come together to enjoy the sport they love.

Our mission is to create a vibrant badminton community by offering top-quality courts, excellent facilities, and innovative technology to help players connect, improve, and enjoy their badminton journey.
